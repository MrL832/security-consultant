    Quick Glossary

    What do these measures mean? (tap to expand)

    Password systems

    How accounts prove identity. Strong passwords + safe storage (hashing) reduce the risk of account takeover.

    Biometrics

    Fingerprint/FaceID can improve usability, but needs a secure fallback and doesn’t remove the need for good account security.

    CAPTCHAs

    Tests designed to block automated bots. Best used when risk is high, not on every action.

    Email confirmations

    Stops some fake signups and helps recovery. Adds friction, so many systems apply it at the right step (e.g., before sensitive actions).

    Automatic updates

    Keeping software patched. The safer approach is staged updates: test, roll out gradually, and be ready to roll back.
